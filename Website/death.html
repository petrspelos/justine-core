Dead, now what?

Death state occurs when your health reaches 0.

This state prevents you from performing actions such as going on missions or digging gold.

Yea, cool. How do I un-die?

Resurrect

Either someone resurrects you for 50 gold with the following command:

Before you try, you cannot resurrect yourself.
